# Attendee Guide

What you can do at a Ziggy kiosk during Experts Live Netherlands 2026. Walk up,
tap, find what you need, walk away.

## The screen layout

Every page sits between two fixed bars:

- **Header (top)** — event logo (tap it to jump to the practical info page), the
  current time, an accessibility menu, and a row of language buttons (NL, EN,
  …).
- **Bottom navigation** — eight icons that switch pages: Now, Agenda, Speakers,
  Map, Expo, Sponsors, Search, Info.

## Pages

### Now (`/now`) — the home screen

Shows sessions happening *right now* and what's coming up next. The screen
auto-refreshes; you don't need to reload anything. Tap a session to see its
description, room, and speakers.

### Agenda (`/agenda`)

The full schedule for the conference. Tabs at the top let you switch between
days. A "jump to now" button takes you to the live timeslot. You can filter the
view by label (e.g. only show "Workshop" or only "Keynote"). Past sessions are
dimmed; the live timeslot is marked with a red **LIVE** pill.

### Speakers (`/speakers`)

A grid of every speaker with photo and tagline. Tap any card to open a profile
with the full bio and the sessions they're presenting.

### Map (`/map`)

The interactive venue floor map. Tap a room to see what's running there now and
what's coming up. From a session, speaker, or sponsor detail screen you can hit
"Show on map" to jump here with the relevant room highlighted in pulsing
amber.

### Expo (`/expo`)

Exhibitor booths with logos and booth numbers. Tap a booth to open its detail
view; if the organizer has linked it to a hotspot, you'll see a "Show on map"
button.

### Sponsors (`/sponsors`)

Tiered sponsor display — Ultimate sponsors at the top in large format, then
Gold, Silver, and so on. Tap a sponsor to open its detail page. Sponsors with a
linked booth or hotspot get a "Show on map" button too.

### Search (`/search`)

Type a query (minimum 4 characters) on the touch keyboard. Results group into
three sections: matching sessions, speakers, and booths. Tap any result to open
its detail.

### Info (`/info`)

Practical event info: WiFi name and password, venue address, event schedule,
emergency contacts, restrooms and other facilities, organizer contact details.

## The accessibility menu

The Ⓐ button in the header opens a small panel with two controls:

- **Text size** — three steps (100% / 120% / 140%) that resize every page.
- **High contrast** — flips to a black background with yellow accents for
  better readability under bright stage lighting.

## Languages

Tap any language button in the header to switch the entire kiosk. Available
languages depend on the event configuration — typically Dutch and English at
Experts Live Netherlands.

## What happens if you walk away

After 60 seconds of no taps the kiosk resets itself: it closes any open detail
view, clears your search, returns to the **Now** screen, and switches the
language back to the default (Dutch). The next attendee always finds a fresh
home screen.
